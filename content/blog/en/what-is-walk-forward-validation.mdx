# What is Walk-Forward Validation?

Every trader who has backtested a strategy knows the feeling: your strategy shows incredible returns on historical data, but the moment you deploy it live, it falls apart. This is **overfitting** — your strategy learned the noise in historical data, not the signal.

## The Problem with Traditional Backtesting

Traditional backtesting optimizes parameters on a fixed dataset. The more parameters you tune, the better your backtest looks — but the less likely it is to work in the future. This is why 95% of strategies that look great in backtesting fail in production.

## How Walk-Forward Validation Works

Walk-forward validation splits your data into multiple segments:

1. **Optimize** on segments 1-3 (in-sample)
2. **Test** on segment 4 (out-of-sample)
3. **Slide** the window forward and repeat

A strategy must be profitable in **at least 3 out of 4 quarters** to pass our validation. This ensures the edge is robust, not a statistical fluke.

## Our Criteria

At AI Crypto Strategy Lab, every strategy goes through:
- Monthly return > 40%
- Maximum drawdown < 40%
- Trading activity in > 50% of weeks
- Walk-forward validation: 3/4 quarters profitable
